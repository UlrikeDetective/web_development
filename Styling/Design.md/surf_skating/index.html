Zen Flow & Breath Hold

Surfing isn't just about movement—it is about breath and calm. Train your lung capacity for wipeouts, and synchronize your movement with calm mental states.

1. Zen Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s, Hold empty 4s. Promotes parasympathetic nervous system activity, lowering heart rate and bringing focused flow state before your street surf session.
2. Surf Wipeout Hold-Down
Three quick breathing cycles to hyper-oxygenate the blood, followed immediately by a 20-second breath hold to simulate a wave washing over you. Teaches you to stay calm under water.
