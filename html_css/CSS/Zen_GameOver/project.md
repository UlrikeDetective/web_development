## Zen Game over

- create a simple game/challenge with html, css (maybe js)

- game/challenge: breathing exercise:

Option one: breath in for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, breath out for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds (repeat 5 times) - then it appears "GAME OVER" - have a wonderful day

Option two: breath in for 6 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, breath out for 6 seconds (repeat 5 times) - then it appears "GAME OVER" - have a wonderful day

Option three: breath in for 7 seconds, breath out for 3 seconds (repeat 5 times) - then it appears "GAME OVER" - have a wonderful day

- color palette: white dove, lotus flower, river stone, bamboo wood, basalt stone, himalayan salt

- use circles

- style: simple elegant, japanese zen style.
